MAKING BETTER CARE POSSIBLE

In our drug distribution centers, we have a common saying: “It’s not just a package, it’s a patient.” We’re in an era of unprecedented complexity in healthcare. Patient care is not where it should be. While many of the organizations delivering the care need to get much healthier, too. This is why our vision is to improve care in every setting — one product, one partner, one patient at a time. And we’re making this happen every day by touching virtually every aspect of healthcare.
